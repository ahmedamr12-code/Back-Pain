Common Causes of Lower Back Pain

  • Non-Mechanical disease processes
  • Heavy lifting
  • Trauma
  • Sciatica
  • Inactivity or prolonged sitting

Relief Tips

  • Apply ice or heat for 15–20 minutes at a time
  • Stay active, avoid bed rest
  • Use over-the-counter pain relievers as directed
  • Improve your posture
  • Use a supportive mattress and ergonomic chair

Recommended Exercises

  • Pelvic tilts
  • Knee-to-chest stretches
  • Cat-cow stretch
  • Bridge exercise
  • Child’s pose (yoga)

Note: Always consult a physical therapist or doctor before starting new exercises.

When to See a Doctor

Seek medical attention if you experience:

  • Severe or persistent pain
  • Numbness or tingling in legs
  • Loss of bladder or bowel control
  • Pain following an injury or fall
